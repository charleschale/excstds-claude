## How to read this guide

The organizing principle is that **especially the best get better.** The Excellence Standards exist because great leaders keep practicing — they never graduate from the behaviors that created the excellence in the first place. This guide is not a test of capability or a report card. It is a practice tool, applied at a specific moment to surface where the next rep lives.

The standards themselves are universal. {{UNIVERSALITY_EXAMPLES}} The instrument is calibrated on workplace data, but the behaviors travel.{{CAREER_STAGE_NOTE}}
